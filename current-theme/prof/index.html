Student Background

Hello! My name is Francis Njenga Mungai from block two village. .

I am sixteen years old. My ambition is to be a doctor so that I may treat sick people in our nation and therefore improve their life expectancy. I went to school at Kiriri Primary School. I worked hard and scored a total of 363 marks. After some months I received a letter that was inviting me to Njoro Boys Highschool. Due to lack of money, I joined Mugumo Highschool. I have both parents and six siblings. My parents are small scale farmers. They really struggle to raise money for my school fees and even getting our daily bread. The scholarship will be of great help to us. My elder siblings were unable to join secondary school. They struggle to make ends meet by doing casual jobs. This scholarship will help me to acquire my ambitions. Since I will be in school. I promise to always work very hard to attain the best grades that will help me join the university and pursue my ambitions. I am very happy and thankful to get this scholarship. I promise to work very hard to ensure I perform very well.
